Dataset yang digunakan: CIFAR-10 (filter kelas airplane dan automobile)

Sumber dataset:
- Nama: CIFAR-10 (Canadian Institute for Advanced Research)
- URL: https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~kriz/cifar.html
- Diakses melalui: torchvision.datasets.CIFAR10 (download otomatis)

Detail:
- Kelas 0: Airplane (pesawat)
- Kelas 1: Automobile (mobil)
- Ukuran gambar: 32x32 piksel, RGB
- Jumlah: 6.000 gambar per kelas (5.000 training, 1.000 test)
- Total setelah filter: 10.000 training (digunakan 70% train, 15% val), 2.000 test

Sumber alternatif jika diperlukan:
- https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abtabm/multiclassimagedatasetairplanecar
- https://huggingface.co/datasets/uoft-cs/cifar10
